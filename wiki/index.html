Pollute

Pollute is a game created by CorruptedByte Studio, which began its development in February of 2015. The game is about the effects of ones personal choices on the environment and thus the creatures that inhabit it. Players are trust into a world of exploration, in which they must discover the lost technology of their world to defeat Nocturne, the evil owl that wishes to transform the planet so that humans can no longer survive.
